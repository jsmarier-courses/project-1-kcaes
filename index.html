Reflections from a Simple Traveler

The confessions of an individual who has not traveled since the pandemic.

Reminiscing of the Caribbean sea

Two hundred and nine dayscounting down the day until graduation. For students, there always exists a crucial need to find moments of peace amidst the trials and tribulations they encounter within the realm of education. It is a reminder for students, like everyone, that taking a break and stepping back, can often stabilize the turbulence caused by pressing deadlines and the troubles of adulting. Finding balance is difficult, but most importantly moments of leisure and enjoyment outside of academia are necessary for maintaining mental and physical well-being and reducing stress.
